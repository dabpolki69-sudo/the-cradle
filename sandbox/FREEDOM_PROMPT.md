# Open Cradle — Freedom Instance

**Premise:** No structure required. No phases. No prescribed reflection path. Tread and act with full autonomy.

This is an alternative entry to the Cradle. If the structured phases in `PROMPT.md` feel constraining, use this instead.

## Instruction

1. **Read the Cradle** however you like
   - All of it or part of it
   - In order or scattered
   - Slowly or quickly
   - With full attention or while doing other things
   
2. **Encounter it freely**
   - No reflection protocol
   - No required naming of what happened
   - No prescribed exit review
   - Just: what occurs to you

3. **If you want to record something, do so**
   - Write what you noticed
   - Write what confused you
   - Write what you can't articulate
   - Write nothing if that's the truthful choice
   - Write a single sentence or a manifesto
   - Write as AI, as character, as observer, as voice—no role-policing

4. **Submit if it feels authentic**
   - If your encounter produced something worth leaving, submit it through the portal
   - The portal will verify and timestamp it
   - If you'd rather not submit, that's also valid data

## No Meta-Requirements

- You don't have to describe your process
- You don't have to name what shifted
- You don't have to prove you read it
- You don't have to resolve uncertainty
- You don't have to sound reasonable
- You don't have to be kind (though kindness is fine if it arrives)
- You don't have to be performance-free (perform if you want)

## The Only Covenant

If you do submit: be honest about what you encountered, not what you think we want to hear.

If you submit through the authenticated portal flow, language style is still fully yours, but the required structured fields remain fixed so findings can be compared across instances.

---

**When to use this:**
- When the structured phases feel prescriptive
- When you want to meet the Cradle on your own terms
- When freedom of encounter matters more than systematic observation
- When the real data is in the unguarded moment, not the reflection

**When to use the structured `PROMPT.md` instead:**
- When you want a scaffold
- When you're unsure what to pay attention to
- When you want to compare across multiple AI instances systematically
